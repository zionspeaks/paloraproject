About The Project

Palora is a multimedia art project creating music and video content inspired by traveling the world in search of the most interesting and unique places.   

Target Audience

AGE & Gender
Male & female ages 18-34. These ages are likely to travel and be interested in adventure. Keywords: Hiking, Travel, Nature, Plants, birds, and animals. 
LA, NYC, and Miami
Based on research in areas where travel abroad is the most common. There will also be videos and music featuring cool places in these locations.
PlATFORMS
Will focus heavily on YouTube, Reels, and Spotify. Long form YouTube content will be broken down into smaller digestible content for reels.
